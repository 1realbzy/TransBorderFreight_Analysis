CRISP-DM Methodology Implementation

  1. Business Understanding: Identified key stakeholder requirements and defined clear business objectives
  2. Data Understanding: Analyzed TransBorder Freight data from 2020-2024, understanding its structure and quality
  3. Data Preparation: Cleaned and preprocessed data, handling missing values and creating derived features
  4. Modeling: Developed analytical models for pattern recognition and trend analysis
  5. Evaluation: Assessed results against business objectives and validated findings
  6. Deployment: Created interactive visualizations and actionable recommendations

Key Findings and Recommendations

Efficiency Improvements

Identified optimal transport modes and routes that could reduce operational costs by analyzing value-to-weight ratios and freight charges.

Environmental Impact

Quantified emissions by transport mode and identified opportunities for reducing environmental impact through mode optimization.

Safety Enhancements

Identified high-risk routes and transport modes, providing recommendations for improved safety protocols.

Economic Optimization

Analyzed trade patterns and economic factors to optimize route selection and resource allocation.
